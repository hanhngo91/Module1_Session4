ABOUT NEW ZEALAND EVISA

New Zealand eVisa is an Electronic Travel Authorization to enter newzealand. New Zealand offers evisa for more than 100+ countries. Travelers can aply for evisa online and recieve electronic travel authrozation in PDF format. Upon arrival in New Zealand travelers can show the printed copy of the visa letter and get their passport stamped.

New Zealand Goverment offers eVisa for Tourism & Business Purpose. The visa type, visa duration & number of entry depends on nationality of the applicants and fixed. Applicants are advised to obtain New Zealand evisa online before travelling to newzealand. E-Visa process is completly online and no paperwork is required. Once New Zealand e-visa is approved it will be sent via email. Applicants have to carry a printed copy of the approved e-visa letter and passport while travelling. Visa letter will be verified by New Zealand officer at the airport and passport will be stamped.

NEW ZEALAND ELECTRONIC TRAVEL AUTHORIZATION CATEGORIES

New Zealand ETA is an Electronic Travel Authorization to enter New Zealand for Tourism purpose. Applicants can obtain New Zealand Electronic Travel Visa by completing a simple & easy eVisa application online. New Zealand ETA is valid for 90 Days or 30 Days depending on type of applicaiton selected.
